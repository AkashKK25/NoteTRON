Frequently Asked Questions

What is NoteTRON?

NoteTRON is a lightweight Note-Taking app designed to be simple yet powerful, with no bloat or unnecessary features allowing you to focus on storing your notes quickly and efficiently.

Is NoteTRON free to use?

Yes! NoteTRON is completely free.

Where are my notes stored?

NoteTRON stores all your notes locally on your device. This ensures privacy and allows you to access your notes even without an internet connection.

Can I transfer my notes between devices?

Yes, you can export your notes as json files and import those notes elsewhere using the same json file.

What are the system requirements?

NoteTRON is very lightweight and should run smoothly on most systems. Minimum requirements: Windows 7+, macOS 10.10+, or Ubuntu 18.04+ (or equivalent Linux distribution).

How do I report bugs or suggest features?

You can report bugs or suggest features through our contact form, by emailing akashkondaparthi@gmail.com, or forking in the GitHub Repo. We value your feedback!
