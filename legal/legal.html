Privacy Policy

Introduction

NoteTRON is a desktop note-taking application designed to be simple and privacy-focused. This privacy policy explains how NoteTRON handles your data.

What Information NoteTRON Collects

NoteTRON is a completely offline application and does not collect any personal information from you. Specifically:

  • NoteTRON does not track your usage
  • NoteTRON does not contain any analytics
  • NoteTRON does not connect to the internet
  • NoteTRON does not upload your notes to any server

How Your Notes Are Stored

All your notes are stored locally on your device in a single JSON file. They are never transmitted to any server or third party.

  • Notes are saved in your user profile directory
  • You have complete control over your data
  • You can backup, delete, or transfer your notes manually as needed

Third-Party Access

Since all data is stored locally on your device, no third parties have access to your notes unless:

  • You manually share your notes with others
  • You allow other users or applications to access your user profile directory

Changes to This Policy

As NoteTRON evolves, this privacy policy may change. Any significant changes will be noted in the application's update notes and on this website.

Conclusion

NoteTRON is designed with privacy in mind. Your notes remain yours alone, and you maintain complete control over your data at all times.
